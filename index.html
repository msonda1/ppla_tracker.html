Core Rules
Top Set
1 × 3–5 @ RPE 8 opens every compound. Does not apply to Arms day (all isolation).
Overload
Double progression — hit top of range on all sets, then +2.5 lb upper / +5 lb lower. Fractional plates to extend runway.
Rest
Top sets: 3 min · working compounds: 2–2.5 min · isolations: 90 sec
Performance
Must hit ≥90% of Set 1 reps on subsequent sets. Extend rest +30 sec if reps drop >2.
Session Cap
19–23 sets typical · Arms max 26 · 6–8 max per muscle
Rep Range
Working sets 6–12 reps · failure on last set of isolations only — never on compounds
Mesocycle
Start lower set counts, add 1 set/exercise every 2 weeks, deload week 5–6
LP
On last set only — 3–5 partial reps in stretched position after full-ROM failure. Larsen 2025: +43% gastroc hypertrophy vs stopping at failure.
Recovery-First Decision Framework
Baselines: RHR ~49 bpm · HRV 7-day avg ~57 ms. Require 2-day persistence before triggering Yellow/Red.
Green
Train normal
HRV within 10% of 7-day avg AND RHR within 5 bpm of baseline.
Yellow
Deload · maintenance kcal
HRV drops 10–15% OR RHR +5–7 bpm.
Red
Full rest · maintenance/surplus
HRV drops >15% AND RHR +7 bpm for 2+ days.
Illness
Pause deficit
RHR +5–10 bpm sustained · RR +1.5–2 bpm for 2+ nights · skin temp +0.5°C for 2+ nights · protein 2.4–2.7 g/kg.
Subjective wellness (fatigue/sleep/soreness/stress/mood z-score) is co-equal to HRV — z ≤ −1.5 for 2+ days defers hard session even if HRV green.
Edge Cases
Alcohol
≥2 drinks = HRV −10 to −25% / RHR +3–5 bpm for 24–48h. NOT overtraining — skip high-intensity next 24h only.
Travel 3–5 zones
Ignore HRV/RHR for 3–5 days. Use subjective + sRPE. Reduce intensity 20–30%.
Travel 6+ zones
Full deload 3–5 days.
Heat Wave
Widen HRV normal range 1.5× for first 7–10 days.
Hydration
Morning USG ≥1.020 + RHR +5 → rehydrate before training.
VO2 Max Protocols · v3.0
Zone 2
Days 1, 2, 6, 7 AM · 35–45 min · bike or incline walk · HR 110–128 bpm · fully conversational. Never running on lifting days.
REHIT
Day 4 AM · 3 × 20-sec all-out sprints on bike · 3 min easy recovery · ~15 min total. Schumann 2022: no interference with hypertrophy.
Nor 4×4
Day 5 PM · 4 × 4 min @ 90–95% HRmax (~165–174 bpm) · 3 min recovery @ ~128 bpm · bike or rower only.
Polarized
~80% Zone 2 / ~20% HIIT per cycle. Avoid Zone 3 "moderate" — polarized beats threshold-only by +11.7% VO2 max.
Interference
6+ hr separation AM cardio ↔ PM lifting. Post-Zone 2: 30–50g carbs + 25–30g protein to blunt AMPK.
Targets
+5–15% at 3mo · +15–25% at 6mo · +20–35% at 12mo from ~37–42 mL/kg/min baseline

About
Volume Load Formula
VL = Sets × Reps × Weight

Approximates mechanical work (F × D) assuming consistent ROM. Well-validated in strength & conditioning literature (Figueiredo et al. 2018; Schoenfeld et al.). For dumbbell/cable work where displacement is consistent session-to-session, VL is the gold standard for tracking progression over time.
